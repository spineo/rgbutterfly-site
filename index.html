RGButterfly

The iPhone App to Suggest Matching Paint Colors

About RGButterfly

This App grew out of my interest in painting. Its main purpose is to suggest Acrylics paint colors associated with selected areas of a photo. It does this by applying a Match Algorithm against a database of reference paints and paint mixes.
